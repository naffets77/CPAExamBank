Updating Your Credit Card Information

If you need to change or want to remove your credit card you can use the forms below. If you upate your card your subscription will not be modified, but the next time it comes to renew your subscriptions your new credit card will be used.

If you remove your card your subscriptions will be set to expire and you'll have access to your questions until the expiration date. You can re-subscribe at anytime to regain access to your questions.

Setting up your Payment Information

Your credit card information is not ever sent to or saved on our servers. We use a third party payment processing plan that manages the security of your credit card information.
